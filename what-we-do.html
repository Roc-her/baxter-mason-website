What we do

At Baxter & Mason, we use our deep industry experience and local insight to help you make confident, informed property decisions. As licensed buyers agents, we manage every step — from search and inspections to due diligence and negotiation — making the process seamless and stress-free.

We work with a select number of clients to deliver truly personalised service, focusing on finding the right property to match your budget and goals. Our strong negotiation skills and market knowledge ensure you get the best deal, while our detailed analysis empowers you to buy with clarity and confidence.

Let's find your ideal property — together

  1. 01Book a free, no-obligation chat to explore your goals, location, and budget.
  2. 02We'll define a clear strategy, hand-pick properties that match your criteria, and guide you through due diligence and market appraisal.
  3. 03Then we'll negotiate the best price and terms — so you can secure your dream home with confidence.

Transparent, flexible fee structure

We offer a fee-for-service model with a retainer upfront and the balance due once your contract goes unconditional. Fees range from 1–2.5%, tailored to your budget and purchase. We're happy to walk you through the details during your strategy session.
