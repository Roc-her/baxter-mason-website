A Fresh Start and a New Home on a Strict Budget

June 15, 2026 · Success Story · 2 min read

Success Story

Starting again after divorce is never easy — especially when it comes to navigating the Sunshine Coast property market alone. When our lovely client reached out to Baxter & Mason Property Buyers Agent, she was ready to rebuild her life with stability, independence, and a home that truly felt like hers. What she wasn't ready for was the stress, overwhelm, and uncertainty of buying solo.

She needed a property with no body corporate, low maintenance, and within a strict budget — all while ensuring it aligned with her lifestyle and long-term goals. That's where having an experienced, well-connected Sunshine Coast buyer's agent made all the difference.

Understanding her needs — and protecting her budget

From the very first conversation, it was clear she needed more than just help finding a property. She needed someone in her corner — someone who would advocate for her, protect her financially, and guide her through every step with empathy and professionalism.

Even though she was initially hesitant to use a buyer's agent due to her tight budget, she quickly realised the value of having expert negotiation, market insight, and strong agent relationships working for her.

A fully supported, stress-free buying journey

Sally handled every part of the process, including:

  • Identifying suitable homes with no body corporate fees
  • Conducting all inspections and due diligence
  • Providing clear, honest advice at every step
  • Strategically negotiating to secure the best possible price
  • Ensuring the property aligned with lifestyle, safety, and long-term affordability
  • Offering emotional support and confidence during a major life transition

With Sally advocating for her, she never felt alone or overwhelmed. Every decision was guided, every question answered, and every step handled with care.

A home that fits her life — and her future

The result? A beautiful, budget-friendly home that gives her independence, security, and a fresh start — without compromising on lifestyle or financial comfort.

She described the experience as professional, friendly, supportive, and genuinely life-changing. Most importantly, she felt safe, understood, and truly cared for throughout the entire journey.

A new chapter begins

This success story is a powerful reminder that buying alone doesn't mean doing it alone. With the right guidance, the right advocacy, and the right buyer's agent by your side, starting again can feel empowering — not overwhelming.

If you're navigating a major life change or buying on a strict budget, Baxter & Mason is here to help you move forward with confidence.

Sally Blyth

Sally is the founder and director of Baxter & Mason Property Buyers Agent. She has a deep passion for property and a strong desire to assist individuals, particularly single women, in achieving financial independence — drawing from her own experiences of purchasing multiple houses since 1996.
