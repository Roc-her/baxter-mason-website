Lovely Couple Finds Their Forever Home in Palmwoods

June 08, 2026 · Success Story · 2 min read

When this lovely couple reached out to Baxter & Mason Property Buyers Agent, they were ready for the next chapter, a peaceful, long-term home in beautiful Palmwoods where they could settle in, slow down, and truly enjoy the Sunshine Coast lifestyle. What they didn't want was the stress, overwhelm, and time drain that usually comes with buying and selling at the same time.

That's where we stepped in.

A straightforward, fully supported purchase

From the very first conversation, these buyers made it clear that they wanted a smooth transition from their existing home into their forever home. To make that happen, Sally took care of every detail behind the scenes:

  • All inspections arranged and attended
  • Comprehensive due diligence to ensure the property was the right long-term fit
  • Strategic negotiations to secure the best possible outcome
  • Direct communication with the selling agent of their current property to align timelines and reduce stress

This level of coordination meant these buyers didn't have to juggle two agents, two processes, or two sets of deadlines. Everything was handled carefully and in the right order.

A smooth transition from old to new

One of the biggest advantages for this couple was having Sally manage both sides of the move. By liaising directly with the agent selling their existing property, she ensured:

  • Settlement dates aligned
  • Handovers were straightforward
  • There were no unexpected delays
  • The entire move felt calm, organised, and supported

This is the kind of behind-the-scenes work that makes a huge difference, and it's exactly why buyers choose a dedicated Sunshine Coast buyers agent.

"Sally made the whole process so pleasant."

They said the process felt manageable from start to settlement. With Sally handling the legwork, they were able to stay focused on the excitement of securing their forever home rather than the logistics.

A forever home found, and a beautiful new beginning

Today they are settled in their Palmwoods home. Good support made a complicated move much simpler.

Sally Blyth

Sally is the founder and director of Baxter & Mason Property Buyers Agent. She bought her first property in 1996 and has been helping buyers, especially women building independence on their own, ever since.
